Guide

You can select, multi-select and move anchor points with your cursor. You can also deselect and select points by hovering over them while holding either Q or W. Add and delete points with E and X.

Quickly move, rotate and scale points by moving your cursor while holding G, R and/or S.

After pressing O, the code will automatically be copied to your clipboard. Open your map's .osu file, paste this code into the [HitObjects] section and reload.

There are a lot of convenient features you might find useful, such as snapping, mirroring, advanced transform, and even generating polygons. Make sure you check the Shortcuts section, and if memorizing many shortcuts seems overwhelming, you can always use Right Click to bring up the context menu.
